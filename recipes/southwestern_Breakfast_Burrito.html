Southwestern Breakfast Burrito

Description

A low carb breakfast that's quick and easy to make. This tasty breakfast has plenty of protein and fiber.

Ingredients

  • 2 low carb whole wheat tortillas
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 1 pinch salt and white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salsa, chunky style
  • 2 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded
