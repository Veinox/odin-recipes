Top Ramen Salad

Description

A ramen noodle salad that is light and crunchy. It's excellent with BBQ chicken. A family favorite recipe!

Ingredients

Salad:

  • 2 (3 ounce) packages chicken-flavored ramen noodles, broken into pieces, seasoning packets served
  • 1/2 cup raw sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 slivered almonds
  • 1 (16 ounce package coleslaw mix)
  • 3 green onions, chopped

Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugare
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
